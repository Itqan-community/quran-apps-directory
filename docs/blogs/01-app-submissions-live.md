## üìã What We're Looking For

When you submit your app, keep these guidelines in mind:

### Quality Standards
- **Functional Islamic Apps** - Quran readers, prayer time apps, Islamic learning tools, etc.
- **Original & Maintained** - Apps should be actively maintained with regular updates
- **Clear Purpose** - Apps should have a clear Islamic or Muslim community benefit
- **Good User Experience** - Apps should be well-designed and user-friendly

### Content Requirements
- **Accurate Descriptions** - Clearly explain what your app does
- **Professional Graphics** - High-quality icons and screenshots
- **Developer Info** - Company or developer name and website
- **Store Links** - At least one link (Google Play, App Store, or other)

### What We Won't List
- Abandoned apps (not updated in 6+ months)
- Apps with poor reviews or known issues
- Non-Islamic apps outside our scope
- Duplicate submissions of the same app
- Apps with misleading descriptions

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: Can I update my submission after sending it?**
A: You can contact us with your tracking ID to request changes before it's approved or rejected.

**Q: What if my app is rejected?**
A: We'll provide clear feedback on why. You're welcome to address the concerns and resubmit.

**Q: What if I need more information?**
A: You'll receive an email explaining what we need. Reply to that email or contact us with your tracking ID.

**Q: How do I find my app after it's approved?**
A: Your app appears immediately in the directory and can be found through search, categories, or developer pages.

**Q: Is there a cost to list my app?**
A: No! Listing is completely free. This is a community-driven directory.

**Q: Can I submit multiple apps?**
A: Yes! Submit as many apps as you'd like. Each gets its own tracking ID.

**Q: Which app stores do you accept?**
A: We accept Google Play Store, Apple App Store, Huawei AppGallery, and other major platforms. At least one store link is required.
